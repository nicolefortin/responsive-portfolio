Georgia Samsa was born and raised in New York City where she spent 20 years in the advertising industry as an art director and creative director for various agencies as well as her own. An ever-changing life prompted her to sit down and evaluate what really inspired her creativity and made her wonder what she would still like to say to the world. Georgia currently lives with her wife and two dogs in San Francisco, California.
